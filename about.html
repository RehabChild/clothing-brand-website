The Origin

Where the name
comes from

RehabChild was born from a deeply personal place — the recognition that healing is not a single moment, but a practice. A child in rehab is not broken. They are in the process of becoming whole.

The brand was created for those who are on that journey. People who have chosen to do the quiet, unglamorous work of returning to themselves. Not loudly. Not for applause. But with intention.

Every piece is designed to hold that space — to be worn through the hard days and the healing ones alike.

i.
Stillness
We design for quiet confidence. Clothes that don't demand attention — they hold it. Pieces that let you exist without announcement.
ii.
Intention
Every fabric choice, every silhouette, every stitch is considered. We believe that conscious creation leads to conscious wearing.
iii.
Growth
RehabChild grows with you. Our pieces are designed to be worn through every stage — the becoming, the being, the returning.

The Founder

Alan C.

Founder & Creative Director

RehabChild began as a personal project — a way to process growth through creativity. What started as sketches became silhouettes, and silhouettes became a brand built for those who understand that the most radical thing you can do is heal slowly, deliberately, and without apology.

The RehabChild Manifesto

01 We believe healing is not linear — and neither is style.
02 We dress for the version of ourselves we are becoming.
03 We choose stillness over noise, intention over impulse.
04 We wear our healing — not as a wound, but as a declaration.
05 We are always, still, becoming.
